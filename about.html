About Us

Learn about who we are!

Protectors of the Earth is a website that aims to spread awareness to people
in hope of preventing the Earth from dying. Our goal is to inspire people to
change their actions to help the world to be a healthier place. We want people
to understand that global warming, deforestation, unnormal climate behaviors,
and animal extinctions are currently happening. As humans, we need to start now.
